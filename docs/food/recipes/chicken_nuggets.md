# Chicken Nuggets

> [!info]
> Serving size: 8-10 pcs
> Prep time: 15 min
> Cooking time:

## Ingredients

- 1 Chicken Breast
- 1/4 cup bread crumbs
- Oil
- Seasonings
- 1 Egg

## Kitchen Items

- Cutting board
- Airfryer
- Knife
- Parchment paper

## Preparation

- Dice chicken breast into nugget size pieces and put in mixing bowl
- Add egg

This is quickly becoming my go-to healthy snack for post-workout. I had previously bought some powdered peanut butter but hadn't used it in a while because I found it missing that extra fat that peanut butter has. Recently I started topping my yogurt bowls with it in powdered form and found it adds a really nice nutty flavour, similar to a graham cracker.

# Ingredients

> [!info]
>Note: You can also use greek yogurt here but I prefer using skyr for its slightly higher protein density.

- 1 cup (~230g) Skyr
- Drizzle of 0 calorie syrup
- 2 tbsp powdered peanut butter
- 1 serving fruits of your choice (bananas, strawberries, blueberries)

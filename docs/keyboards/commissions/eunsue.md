I was lucky enough to be commissioned for the first time from my two close friends Eunwoo and Sue! Being my first time building keyboards for my friends and having briefly chatted with them about what they were interested in, I wanted to get a deeper understanding of what they wanted for their first custom keyboards.

# The Questionnaire

I prepared a "quick" survey breaking down some of the common bigger areas and tried to keep it as simple as possible. I broke it down into the following sections:

- General information: budget, operating system, etc
- Size: common footprints for keyboards (think 60%, TKL, full)
- Layouts: staggered (ISO, ANSI), ortho-linear, 
- Aesthetics: case colours, keycap colours / design / legends
- Switches: sound and feel

![Survey]()

# The Searching Begins

After getting the survey results in, and some back and forth discussions with Eunwoo and Sue, I started looking at what we can get that fits their respective needs. I did this by starting to compile lists of each thing we will have to buy (namely keyboard kit/case, keycaps and switches).

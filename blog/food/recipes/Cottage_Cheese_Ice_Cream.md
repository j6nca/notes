# Cottage Cheese Ice Cream

Tags: dessert, low_carb, protein

# Notes

Serving size: ~ 1 pint

# Ingredients

- 16oz Cottage Cheese
- 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
- 1/3 cup Sweetener
- 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder

# Kitchen Items

Blender

# Preparation

- Toss all ingredients into blender, blend for 1.5 min
- Throw in freezer overnight
